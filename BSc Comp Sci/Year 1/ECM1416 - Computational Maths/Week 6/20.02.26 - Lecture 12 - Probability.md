##### Standard Deviation
 - Denoted by $\sigma$
 - $\sigma_{X} = \sqrt{ Var(X) }$.


##### Properties of Variance
 - $Var(cX) = c^2Var(X)$
 - $Var(X+Y) = Var(X) + Var(Y) + 2Cov(X,Y)$


##### What is Covariance?
 - The covariance ($Cov(X,Y)$) between two random variables $X$ and $Y$ is a measure of dependency between $X$ and $Y$.
 - Formula: $Cov(X,Y) = E(XY) - E(X)E(Y)$.
 - If $X$ and $Y$ are independent, $Cov(X,Y) = 0$.
 - Therefore, $Var(X+Y) = Var(X) + Var(Y)$.

##### Bernoulli Distribution
 - Trials:
	 - Are independent.
	 - Have the same probability.
	 - Outcomes are sequences of successes and failures (e.g. item is defective or not).
 - The Probability Mass Function (PMF): $P(X=x) = p^x(1-p)^{1-x}$.
 - Say $x=0$ is failure and $x=1$ is success:
	 - When $x=0$: $P(X=x) = 1-p$.
	 - When $x=1$: $P(X=x) = p$.
 - Mean:
	 - $E(X) = \sum_{i=0,1}x_{i}P(X=x_{i}) = (1*P(X=1)) + (0*P(X=0)) = p$.
