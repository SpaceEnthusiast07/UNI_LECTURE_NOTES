##### Quicksort History
 - Invented by English Scientist Sir C.A.R. (Tony) Hoare in 1961.


##### Quicksort
 - In place manipulation.
 - Uses small stack as auxiliary structure
 - It is a divide and conquer algorithm
	 - I.e. split the problem up, solve each sub-problem and then combine the results to solve the original problem.


##### Quicksort Algorithm
 - Original idea in 1961:
	 1. Pick one item from the list and call it `pivot`.
	 2. Partition
