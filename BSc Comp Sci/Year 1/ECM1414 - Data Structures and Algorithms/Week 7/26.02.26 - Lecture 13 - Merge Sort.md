##### What is a Stable Sort?
 - A stable sort algorithm is one which can be applied to a sorted list and the order will remain the same.


##### Merge Sort Algorithm
 - Calculate the index of the middle of the list and call it m
 - Use recursion to deal with the two partitions: `[first, m]` and `[m+1,last]`.
