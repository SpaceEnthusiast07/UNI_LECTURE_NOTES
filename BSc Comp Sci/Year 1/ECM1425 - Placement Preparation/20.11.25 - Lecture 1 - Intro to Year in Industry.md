##### Why is it important?
 - You get paid
 - Increases your chance of getting a job after you graduate (sets you ahead from the competition)
 - Placement employer may offer you a job after you graduate - reducing stress during year 4 or 5

 - It can improve your academic performance for year 4! - students come back much more confident and knowledgeable
